League Format & Rules
Please read all rules carefully before agreeing.
  1. Arrive 5 minutes early. If late by more than 5 minutes, you will be moved to the last court.
  2. Warm-up is limited to 5 minutes at the start and must be done only on your assigned court. Do not use new shuttlecocks for warm-up — use old/used ones only. Once games begin, no more warm-ups are allowed.
  3. Each court is allotted 8 shuttlecocks (2 per player). Extra shuttlecocks must be provided by players.
  4. Games are played to 21 points. First to reach 21 wins, even at 20-20. No deuce.
  5. Scores are entered by one person per court after confirming with all players. The app shows exactly who plays who in each game (Game 1: A+B vs C+D · Game 2: A+C vs B+D · Game 3: A+D vs B+C).
  6. The top scorer per court moves UP, the lowest scorer moves DOWN each round — no exceptions.
  7. Remaining shuttlecocks are distributed to top scorers per court starting from Court 1.
  8. New shuttlecocks are ONLY for official ladder matches. Use old/used shuttlecocks while waiting between rounds or during casual play.
  9. The court where you finish each session is your starting court the following week. Court positions carry over week to week.
  10. All court allocations are final and decided by the admin. If a higher-caliber spare player joins, you may be displaced from your court position.
  11. Maintain respectful communication and resolve disputes through the admin only. League polls may be conducted via the Facebook group.
  12. Missing a session without notice will move your court one down at the start of the following session. Repeated unexcused absences may result in losing your spot to a waitlist player.
  13. Bring your own water bottle and towel. No glass containers in the gymnasium. Wear non-marking indoor shoes at all times.
  14. Each session has multiple rounds. The number of rounds depends on available time and is decided by the admin. All courts must complete their games before the next round begins.
  15. If a court has fewer than 4 players (due to absences or odd numbers), it switches to singles round-robin format. 3 players play 3 singles games; 2 players play best-of-3 singles.
  16. At the end of each session, remaining shuttlecocks on each court are distributed to the top scorer(s) on that court.
  17. Registration through this app confirms your interest and waiver acceptance. Court placement is handled separately by the admin — registering does not automatically assign you to a court.
  18. The app tracks all scores, rankings, and court movements in real time. Players can view standings, game history, and session results at any time.
I have read and agree to all of the league format rules listed above.
